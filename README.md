# Sales-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using Tableau)

## Data-Analysis-Dashbaord

This interactive Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, profit, and quantity performance with a strong focus on year-over-year comparison and trend analysis
## Data Used
- <a href="https://github.com/Ahsan-Awan123/Data-Analysis-Dashbaord/blob/main/Project-Dataset.xls">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
-How are total sales trending over time, and how do they compare year-over-year?  
-Is revenue growth translating into profit growth, or is margin being compressed?  
-Which periods show above-average vs below-average performance in sales and profit?  
-Is growth driven by higher sales volume (quantity) or by pricing effects?  
-Which product subcategories contribute the most to sales and profit?  
-Are there high-sales subcategories that are consistently unprofitable?  
-How does current-year performance compare to the prior year across subcategories?  
-Which products or time periods represent the highest risk to profitability?
